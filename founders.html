Meet the Founders

Randal S. Olson, Ph.D. ("Randy")

Co-Founder

Randy is a systems builder with 15+ years in AI and engineering. His background spans a Ph.D. at Michigan State University focused on creating AI and years architecting data-driven solutions for organizations ranging from startups to large enterprises. At Wyrd Studios, he owns the AI stack and product strategy. He turns messy, human workflows into reliable automation that gives people more control over their digital lives.

His approach is pragmatic and human-centered: ship useful automation, measure real outcomes, and keep humans in the driver's seat. He prioritizes sustainable value over hype, designing calm technology that reduces cognitive load, cuts toil, and compounds over time.

"Amplify what humans do best. Automate the rest. That's how we build technology that works for people, not the other way around."

Key Expertise: AI Strategy & Architecture, Data-Driven Decision Making, Human-Centered Technology Design

Olivia Rose Olson

Co-Founder

Olivia is an experience architect who designs clarity from chaos. She has degrees in Cognitive Science and Design Engineering, with a focus on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and AI. Her career has spanned startups, large tech companies, and government. She specializes in making complex, ambiguous systems feel simple and enjoyable for the people who use them. At Wyrd Studios, she owns the user experience, product design, and service design strategy.

Her approach puts the human at the center of every system. She finds where people get lost, designs for intuition, and ships experiences that build trust. Whether modernizing large-scale enterprise systems or refining the smallest interactions, her focus is always on prioritizing clarity over clutter. The goal is to create inclusive, calm technology that reduces friction, sparks curiosity, and empowers users to navigate complexity with confidence.

"So much of modern technology is designed to exploit our focus and attention. We build tools that respect your attention, serve your goals, and leave you in control."

Key Expertise: Strategic CX Leadership, Service Design & Modernization, Cross-Cultural UX Research, Behavioral Science Applications

Shared Vision

Randy and Olivia founded Wyrd Studios on shared growth mindsets and the conviction that technology should empower rather than exploit. Their complementary skills - Randy's systematic AI approach and Olivia's empathetic design methodology - create products users trust and competitors respect.

Our commitment is structural. By operating on a subscription model, we ensure that you are our customer, not the product we sell to advertisers. This approach is our invitation to build a more intentional and rewarding relationship with information and is the first, essential step in our larger mission to build technology that truly serves humanity.
