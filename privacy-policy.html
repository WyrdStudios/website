Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2025
Welcome to Wyrd Studios. We take privacy seriously - it's core to our mission of building human-centered technology.

What this means: Here's who's responsible for your data.

Contact us at: privacy@thewyrdstudios.com

2. What We Collect

What this means: We collect minimal information to provide our services.

Newsletter Signups

  • Email address (required for newsletter delivery)

3. How We Use Your Information

What this means: We use your information to provide our services and improve your experience.

We use your information to:

  • Send newsletter updates about our projects and workshops
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

What this means: We work with trusted partners to provide our services.

We don't sell your data. We share it only with:

  • Our email service provider (MailerLite) for newsletter delivery
  • Our website hosting provider for site functionality

All third-party services are bound by strict data protection agreements.

5. Cookies and Tracking

What this means: We use minimal, privacy-respecting tracking.

We use essential cookies for:

  • Website functionality and security
  • Theme preference storage

We do not use marketing cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics.

6. Your Rights and Choices

What this means: You have control over your data.

You can:

  • Unsubscribe from newsletters at any time
  • Request deletion of your data by emailing us
  • Request a copy of your data

7. Data Security

What this means: We protect your data with industry-standard security measures.

We implement:

  • Secure hosting and infrastructure
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access controls for staff

8. Data Retention

What this means: We keep your data only as long as necessary.

We retain:

  • Newsletter data: Until you unsubscribe or request deletion

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

10. International Transfers

Your data is processed in the United States. If you are located outside the U.S., please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the U.S. where our servers are located.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@thewyrdstudios.com

Address: Wyrd Studios, Inc., Anacortes, WA, USA
